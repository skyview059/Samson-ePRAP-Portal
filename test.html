Approach

 

Assess the patient using ABCDE algorithm

 

A

  • Hello xyz, How are you feeling? What brought you to the hospital today? 

  • Can you cough for me?

  • He is talking in full sentences

 

B - Breathing

  • I now need to assess your breathing

  • I can see your oxygen saturation is low, 93% on air

  • Do you smoke?

  • Have you ever been diagnosed with COPD

  • I will give you high flow of oxygen via reserver bag (Non rebreathing bag)

  • Place oxygen mask on the patients face

  • And then ask the patient How do you feel?

  • Look at the monitor and say you can see that the oxygen has improved

 

Take a quick history

  • Do you have any medical conditions

  • Any shortness of breath/pain/dizziness/lightheadedness or feeling faint

  • Take a history of per rectal bleed

  • When did you start bleeding

  • Is it bright red blood or dark blood

  • Any black stool recently 

  • Has this happened before

  • Any recent procedures such as endoscopy or any surgeries

  • Why did you undergo colonoscopy

  • Any stomach ulcers

  • Any weight loss, tired or fatigue?

  • Any medical conditions such as High BP? High Blood sugar?

  • Any regular medication

  • How long have you been taking warfarin?

  • What condition do you have?

  • Why did you have a colonoscopy?

  • What symptoms were you experiencing?

  • What were the results of the colonoscopy?

  • Did they tell you what was wrong with you?
